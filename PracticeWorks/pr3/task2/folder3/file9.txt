Music has the power to move, inspire, and connect people across cultures and generations. From classical symphonies to contemporary pop hits, music expresses emotions and tells stories in a way that transcends language. The evolution of music has been shaped by technological advancements, such as the invention of musical instruments, recording devices, and streaming platforms. Genres like jazz, rock, and hip-hop have transformed the music industry, introducing unique rhythms, melodies, and themes. Music therapy is now recognized for its ability to improve mental health, reduce stress, and enhance cognitive function.
