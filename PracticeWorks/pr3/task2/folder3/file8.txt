The history of art is a journey through the evolution of human expression. From prehistoric cave paintings to modern abstract masterpieces, art reflects the values, beliefs, and experiences of different cultures. The Renaissance marked a pivotal moment in art history, with figures like Leonardo da Vinci and Michelangelo pushing the boundaries of creativity and technical skill. The Impressionist movement in the 19th century introduced a new way of capturing light and movement, influencing countless artists. Today, art continues to evolve, blending traditional techniques with digital technologies to create innovative forms of expression.
