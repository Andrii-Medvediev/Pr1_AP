Artificial intelligence is transforming industries around the world. From healthcare to finance, AI-powered tools are automating tasks and providing insights that were previously unattainable. Machine learning, a subset of AI, enables systems to improve over time by analyzing data. Despite its benefits, AI raises ethical questions about privacy, job displacement, and bias in algorithms. Striking a balance between innovation and regulation is crucial to ensuring that AI serves humanity responsibly. The future of AI will depend not just on technological advancements but also on the frameworks we create to govern its use.
