The rise of e-commerce has transformed the retail industry, making shopping more convenient than ever. Online platforms allow consumers to browse, compare, and purchase products from the comfort of their homes. Advances in logistics and payment systems have further streamlined the e-commerce experience. However, challenges like cybersecurity risks, counterfeit goods, and environmental concerns remain. As the industry continues to grow, businesses must adapt to changing consumer preferences and prioritize sustainability. The future of retail lies in the seamless integration of online and offline experiences.
